RELATIONSHIP SUMMARY
====================

1. SKU_INVENTORY_DATA (1) ←→ (M) DEMAND_DATA
   - Join Key: Oracle Item Number
   - Additional Join: Deliver To = Deliver to Location
   - Coverage: 100% overlap (every SKU has demand data)

2. SKU_INVENTORY_DATA (1) ←→ (0..1) REMOVED_SKUS
   - Join Key: Oracle Item Number
   - Purpose: Audit trail for removed SKUs
   - Coverage: 8.8% of original SKUs removed

DATA QUALITY CONSTRAINTS
========================

✅ SKU Format: All SKUs zero-padded to 6 characters (e.g., '000005')
✅ Referential Integrity: Every SKU in DEMAND_DATA exists in SKU_INVENTORY_DATA
✅ No Orphaned Records: All relationships properly maintained
✅ Data Validation: All numeric fields within valid ranges
✅ Time Consistency: Demand data covers 5.5+ years of history

LOCATION HIERARCHY
==================

Hospital System (14 Locations)
